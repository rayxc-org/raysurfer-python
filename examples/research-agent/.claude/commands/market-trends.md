Research current market trends in: $ARGUMENTS

## Trend Analysis Framework

### 1. Current State of the Market
- Market size and growth rate
- Key players and market share
- Recent significant events

### 2. Emerging Trends (Top 5-7)
For each trend:
- What is it?
- Why is it happening now?
- Who is driving it?
- What's the evidence?
- Projected impact

### 3. Technology Trends
- New technologies entering the market
- Technology adoption curves
- Disruption potential

### 4. Consumer/Business Behavior
- Shifting preferences
- New use cases
- Changing expectations

### 5. Regulatory and Policy
- New regulations
- Policy changes
- Compliance requirements

### 6. Investment and Funding
- Where money is flowing
- VC trends
- M&A activity

### 7. Expert Predictions
- Analyst forecasts
- Industry leader opinions
- Conference themes

## Research Approach

Spawn researchers for:
- Industry reports and analysis
- News and recent developments
- Expert commentary and predictions
- Data and statistics

## Output Format

Structure the report as:
1. Executive Summary (key trends at a glance)
2. Detailed Trend Analysis
3. Implications and Recommendations
4. Sources and Methodology

Include a "Trend Confidence Score" (High/Medium/Low) for each identified trend.
